Why Research?

Empathy. Also, we are not our users.

I like to use research as a tool to help discover what users want so that we're not operating off assumptions.

I find that there are three main reasons to do user research:

  • To create relevant experiences
  • To create experiences that are enjoyable to use
  • To understand the ROI of created experiences
Of course, these three reasons are a cycle; one is not more important than the other.
